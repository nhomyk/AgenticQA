## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data encrypted in database
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and penetration testing
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 monitoring for unauthorized access attempts
- **Incident Response:** Documented breach response procedures

**Limitation:** While we implement robust security, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the EU, you have the following rights under GDPR:

### 7.1 Right to Access
You can request access to personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Right to Rectification
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a portable, structured format to transfer elsewhere.

### 7.5 Right to Restrict Processing
You can request limitation on how we use your data.

### 7.6 Right to Object
You can object to our processing, particularly for marketing communications.

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent for processing at any time.

### 7.8 Filing a Complaint
You have the right to file a complaint with your local supervisory authority (data protection authority).

**How to Exercise GDPR Rights:**  
Contact us at privacy@agentic-qa.com with "GDPR REQUEST" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.
