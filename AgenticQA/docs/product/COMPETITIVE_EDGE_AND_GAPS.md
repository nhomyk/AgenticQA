# Competitive Edge and Market Gap

## Market gap to own
Most quality/CI tools provide alerts and dashboards but do not provide a rigorous **baseline→delta ROI system of record** tied to actionable recommendations and execution pathways.

## Core edge
1. **Proof-first architecture**
   - Every recommendation ties to measurable trend movement.
2. **Cross-tool portability**
   - Works across heterogeneous repo/CI stacks.
3. **Compounding intelligence**
   - Historical evidence and outcomes improve recommendation relevance over time.
4. **Natural execution bridge**
   - Prompt-to-workflow and Autonomous Repair can act directly on identified deltas.

## Defensibility levers
- Evidence graph of interventions vs outcomes
- Repo/team benchmark corpus
- Governance and audit trail maturity for enterprise buyers
- Integration depth in customer CI/dev workflow

## Product positioning statement
"AgenticQA is the quality ROI operating system: it proves where delivery quality is changing, why, and what action to take next—then automates improvement." 

## Capability hierarchy
1. Measure (scorecard + baseline)
2. Explain (evidence-linked insights)
3. Recommend (prioritized actions)
4. Execute (workflow automation / repair)
5. Govern (enterprise controls)
