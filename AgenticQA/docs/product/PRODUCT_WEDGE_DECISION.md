# Product Wedge Decision: Engineering Quality ROI OS

## Decision (final)
Build the first commercial product around the **Baseline→Delta Portability Scorecard + Evidence Layer**.

This is the fastest path to a defensible wedge because it proves measurable value in language buyers approve.

## Product name (working)
**AgenticQA ROI OS**

## Core Promise
"In 14 days, we produce a baseline→delta quality improvement report for your repos, with prioritized actions and traceable evidence."

## Why this wedge wins
1. Budget-aligned value: translates quality operations into measurable business outcomes.
2. Fast onboarding: repo profiling + CI/test signal ingestion works in hours, not quarters.
3. Data moat: historical baseline/delta/evidence timeline is proprietary and compounds.
4. Expansion path: once trusted, customers adopt automation and autonomous repair to improve score outcomes.

## MVP Scope (must ship first)
- Repo portability scorecard (overall + sub-scores)
- Baseline snapshot API + persistence
- Delta trend classification (`improved`, `regressed`, `flat`)
- Quick-win recommendations
- Exportable weekly ROI report (JSON/CSV/markdown summary)
- Team and repo rollups for leadership visibility

## Non-goals for MVP
- Full autonomous remediation loops across all stacks
- Deep compliance suite breadth
- Broad marketplace integrations before core KPI loop is proven

## ICP (initial)
- Engineering Managers
- Platform Engineering teams
- QA/Quality Engineering leads
- Mid-market SaaS teams with active CI and recurring flaky/failure issues

## Success Metrics (30-60 days)
- Time-to-first-baseline < 1 day
- Weekly active scorecard viewers
- % repos with improving trend after 2 sprints
- Reduction in CI failure and rerun rates
- Expansion conversion to Pro automation features

## Commercial packaging intent
- Free: baseline visibility and core dashboard
- Pro: advanced analytics + workflow automation + team intelligence
- Enterprise: governance/security/compliance + private deployment

## Expansion products (after wedge)
1. Autonomous Repair Engine (premium add-on)
2. Compliance & Audit Evidence Suite (enterprise add-on)
3. Prompt Ops at scale (team/enterprise upsell)
