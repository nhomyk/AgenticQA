# AgenticQA Sales One-Pager (Draft)

## What AgenticQA does
AgenticQA helps software teams move from reactive quality firefighting to proactive, autonomous quality operations.

## Ideal customer profile
- Engineering leaders responsible for release quality and CI stability
- QA/Quality Engineering teams driving pipeline reliability
- Platform teams standardizing delivery governance
- Regulated organizations needing auditability and control

## Core value
- Faster issue detection and recovery
- Lower CI flake/failure churn
- Better engineering focus through reduced manual triage
- Clear baseline→delta ROI story for leadership

## Packaging summary
### Free / Community
Low-friction onboarding for local-first evaluation and early wins.

### Pro / Team
Team-scale automation, analytics, and collaboration intelligence.

### Enterprise
Security, governance, compliance, and deployment flexibility for scale.

## Add-ons
- RAG+ Intelligence
- Autonomous Repair
- Prompt Ops Automation
- Compliance Suite

## Objection handling (short)
- **"We already have CI tooling."** AgenticQA augments existing CI with quality intelligence, routing, and automation.
- **"Will setup be heavy?"** Free tier is designed for fast onboarding with minimal friction.
- **"Can we meet security/compliance needs?"** Enterprise includes governance controls and private deployment paths.

## Discovery questions
1. Which quality bottleneck causes the most delivery delay today?
2. How are CI failures and flaky tests currently triaged?
3. What compliance or audit constraints apply to your repos/pipelines?
4. Which KPI will define success in the first 30–60 days?

## Suggested next step
Run a scoped baseline in one pipeline, capture baseline→delta after two sprints, then expand.
